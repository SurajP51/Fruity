PRODUCTS

Apple

Apples are popular and nutritious fruits. They come in various colors, with a crisp or juicy texture.Apples promote digestive health and may reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Orange

Oranges are excellent source of vitamin C, dietary fiber, and antioxidants. Oranges are commonly consumed fresh, juiced, or used in various culinary applications.

Strawberry

Strawberries are small, red fruits packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants. They have a sweet and tangy flavor, and are commonly used in desserts, jams, and smoothies.

Grapes

Grapes are small, juicy fruits that grow in clusters on vines. They come in various colors and flavors, are rich in antioxidants, and can be eaten fresh or used for making wine.

Pomegranate

Pomegranate is a fruit known for its high antioxidant content. It is rich in vitamins and minerals, offering potential health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and cardiovascular support.

Mango

Mango is a tropical fruit known for its sweet, juicy, and fragrant flesh. It is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, and is enjoyed in various culinary dishes and desserts.
